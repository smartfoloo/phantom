Concerned about being restricted from Pantom? You can effortlessly rehost Pantom on a different domain at no extra cost by using Freedns or your own domain. Here’s how it works:

  1. Create a Freedns Account: Sign up at Freedns with a real email address for verification.
  2. Login and Access the Registry: After logging in, navigate to the “Registry” section.
  3. Select a Domain: Choose a domain with fewer hosts to minimize the risk of your link being blocked.
  4. Create a Subdomain: Follow the prompts to set up a subdomain (e.g., mycoollink.yourchosendomain.com) pointing to the Pantom's IP.
  5. Wait for Activation: It may take 5 minutes to 1 hour for your new link to activate.
  6. Share Your Link: Once active, you can share your link with others.

For details on Pantom's IP and a video tutorial, join our Discord community.

If you're not interested in creating your own link, check out the link dispenser in our Discord for personal links in one click!
